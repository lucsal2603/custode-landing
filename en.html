FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need Beds24 or a channel manager?

If you already have one, we connect Custode in minutes. If you don't, we handle everything: connecting to the portals (Airbnb, Booking…) is included in the price — you set up nothing.

Is it safe to let an AI answer my guests?

Yes. The agent only answers simple questions on its own (check-in, wifi, times…). For discounts, problems or sensitive cases it doesn't decide alone: it alerts you with a notification and a ready-made draft, and you confirm.

Can I pause it whenever I want?

Yes, with one click. While paused, guest requests come straight to you, with no automatic replies, until you turn it back on.

What languages does it reply in?

In the guest's language, automatically: English, Italian, German, French, Spanish and more. If a guest writes in English, it replies in English.

What if it doesn't know an answer?

It never makes things up. If some info isn't in your property sheet, it checks with you before replying instead of guessing.

How much is it and how do I cancel?

€39 a month, with all your properties included (however many). No lock-in: cancel anytime.
